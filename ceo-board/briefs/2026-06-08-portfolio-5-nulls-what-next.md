# Portfolio: 5 Edge-Searches, 5 Honest Nulls — Wind Down, Pivot Regime, or Bank as R&D?

## Situation

Over months, every rigorous quant edge-search across the portfolio has independently returned a NULL under honest OOS / write-once-holdout / CLV / FDR / cost validation:
- Atlas equities — momentum, 22 technical strategies, fundamentals (value/quality + value/momentum), plus a 0.986-DSR candidate that FAILED the write-once holdout. A 23-family failure diagnostic this session confirmed the system is EDGE-bound, not gate-bound (well-powered broad books, ~0 edge).
- Atlas crypto — null.
- Midas — best illiquidity+carry pair misses the strict DSR bar by 0.06.
- Cronus — 48 candidate edges found then archived.
- Hermes — full-game MLB dead OOS at realistic commission; pitcher-K props (the LAST untested "soft corner": richest data edge [Statcast] x softest market [player props]) = clear, well-powered screen null (CLV +0.17% p=0.056, ROI -9.7% on 1,749 bets; 2025 holdout never burned).

5 domains, 5 nulls. We have now tested the strongest version of every "but maybe HERE" hypothesis — better data (paid Sharadar point-in-time fundamentals; paid historical prop odds), softer market (player props vs full-game), orthogonal signal (fundamentals vs price; insider events scoped). The shared research-integrity rails repeatedly caught mirages and kept capital off noise. Compute is free; binding constraints are human attention, deployable capital, and integrity. Nothing is deployed live anywhere. The genuine assets produced: the methodology + honest negative knowledge. This session alone, "verify before committing" prevented ~5 overspends.

## Stakes

The allocation of the operator's scarcest resources — attention and any future capital — going forward. Continuing retail-scale edge-hunting has EV ~0 (5/5 nulls is strong evidence; the FDR bar is now ~0.98 and rising with each look, making new "passes" mathematically self-defeating). A regime pivot (intraday/microstructure, private data, market-making) has potential upside but needs material capital/infra (likely $1K-$10K+ and months) with uncertain payoff, new operational risk, and a base-rate prior that even institutional edges are hard. Mis-allocating here wastes months of the operator's life. Opportunity cost: that time could fund an entirely different venture. What is genuinely valuable and reusable (the rails + methodology + negative knowledge) is at risk of being undervalued if we frame this purely as "failure."

## Constraints

Retail scale (~$1-5K live equity slices, small AUM). Free compute but severely scarce human attention. NO access to: co-located/low-latency infra, institutional-cost private/alternative data, market-making rebates, or large capital. Operator is AU-based (venue + regulatory limits — e.g., pitcher-K props are not cleanly bankable; US books limit winners). Paid data is gated and one-time download-and-own only (~$100 total spent this cycle across SF1 + Odds API, both now cancellable). The research-integrity rails are non-negotiable for any future work. Revenue-honesty guardrail stands: no capital into modest-EV; deploy only on holdout-cleared + forward-confirmed edges with an explicit "worth it" check.

## Key Questions

1. Given 5/5 independent honest nulls and a rising FDR bar, is retail-scale edge-hunting on liquid/semi-liquid markets empirically EXHAUSTED (EV ~0), such that active factor/strategy generation should formally STOP?
2. Is there a fundamentally DIFFERENT regime — intraday/microstructure, genuinely private/alternative data, or market-making vs taking — that plausibly contains edge AND is reachable at our scale/capital/infra? If yes, which single one, and what is the minimum-viable probe with pre-committed kill criteria?
3. What is the honest value of what has been built (the research-integrity methodology + the corpus of negative knowledge), and is there a credible path to monetize/deploy the METHODOLOGY itself (service, research product, open infra) rather than the alpha?
4. Should the portfolio be formally declared a completed R&D exercise — wind down active research, maintain the rails, cancel paid data — and if so, where should the operator's freed attention go?
5. If we pivot regimes, what capital/infra/time budget is justified given the base rate, and what is the PRE-COMMITTED stop that prevents throwing good money/time after the 5-null pattern?
